About Amity University Rajasthan

Top-ranked Private University in Rajasthan

Welcome to Amity University Rajasthan

Where a commitment to academic excellence, global exposure, and innovation meets the scenic beauty of the Aravali mountains. Established in 2008 under the Amity University Rajasthan Act, AUR has rapidly ascended as a top-ranked private university, blending rigorous academics with value-based learning, preparing students for the demands of the modern world.

Our Campus

Spread across 152 lush acres, Amity University Rajasthan offers an expansive, eco-friendly campus equipped with state-of-the-art learning and recreational facilities. With over 120 high-tech labs, dedicated research centers, a comprehensive library, and vibrant student spaces, AUR cultivates an environment where knowledge, discipline, and recreation come together.

A Unique Academic Approach

Our academic programs focus on both depth and flexibility, beginning with foundational studies and advancing towards specialized, career-ready skills. Students benefit from experiential learning, with numerous internships, research projects, and value-added courses that enhance their employability. We emphasize holistic development, with programs tailored to industry demands, a cross-cultural exchange of knowledge, and mentorship from over 225 distinguished faculty members.

Extra-Curricular and Co-Curricular Excellence

At AUR, we believe in grooming well-rounded individuals. Our expansive sports facilities, military training program, and diverse clubs—ranging from dramatics to environmental protection—offer students opportunities to develop beyond academics. Signature events like Sangathan and Amity Leadership Fest foster leadership and camaraderie.

A Legacy of Innovation and Research

Amity University Rajasthan is dedicated to advancing research with initiatives spanning climate studies, nanotechnology, biotechnology, and more. Our partnerships with international institutions enable a global exchange of knowledge, furthering our commitment to impactful research and development.

Global Alumni Network and Placement Support

Our Corporate Resource Center bridges the gap between academia and industry, creating robust placement pathways with prestigious companies such as TCS, IBM, Citibank, and Infosys. AUR’s strong alumni network offers ongoing mentorship and opportunities, propelling graduates into successful careers both in India and abroad.
